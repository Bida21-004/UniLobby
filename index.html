WHAT IS UNI LOBBY

UniLobby is a web application designed to help prospective students select courses based
on their grades and gives them insights on which universities that offer those courses.

The web application is designed to help students in course selection.

The system aims to provide the best possible results for course selection for inexperienced prospects to make.

UniLobby aims to incorporate these factors and provide personalized course recommendations to help students make informed decisions.

By leveraging these research findings, UniLobby can provide a valuable resource to students in their academic journey
